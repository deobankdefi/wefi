The WeFi Story

How we're building the world's first decentralized on-chain bank

In September 2024, a revolutionary idea became reality. WeFi launched as the world's first Deobank (Decentralized On-Chain Bank), fundamentally changing how people interact with money. Unlike traditional banks that control your funds or neobanks that simply digitize old systems, WeFi operates entirely on blockchain technology, giving you true ownership and control.

Our vision is simple yet powerful: Financial access is a fundamental human right. Everyone deserves the freedom to control their money, regardless of where they live or how wealthy they are.

At WeFi, we believe the outdated traditional banking model has failed billions of people. That's why we're building something better. A system that's inclusive, transparent, and built on the principles of decentralization. With advanced AI-driven compliance and Zero-Knowledge Proofs, we bring institutional-grade security while maintaining your privacy.

Today, WeFi serves a global community with features like crypto-collateralized Visa cards, custodial and self-custody wallets, stablecoin management, global remittances, and AI-powered compliance. We're not just building a platform. We're creating a financial revolution that empowers 1.4 billion unbanked individuals worldwide.
